Affect of Technology in Environment

Technology has affect environment in many ways. Air and water pollution which means excessive discharge of harmful gases like carbon dioxide, carbon monoxide, sulfur dioxide, nitric oxide and methane are introduced into the earth’s atmosphere. And most of the source of these gases are relates to technology. Not only this, technology has also plays vital role in depletion of natural resources. It refers to the consumption of a resource faster than it can be replenished.

Technology also has good aspects, natural renewable sources are converted into electrical or useful heat through devices such as solar panels, wind and water turbines, which reflects a highly positive impact of technology on the environment. Smart home technology uses devices such as linking sensors and other appliances connected to the Internet of Things (IoT) that can be remotely monitored and programmed in order to be as energy efficient as possible and to respond to the needs of the users.
